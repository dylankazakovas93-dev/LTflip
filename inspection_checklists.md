# Inspection Checklists

## Camera lens inspection

Bring a compatible camera body where possible.

Reject if:
- seller refuses testing
- visible fungus/haze
- deep scratches
- autofocus fails
- aperture blades stick
- zoom/focus ring grinds badly
- mount is loose/damaged
- serial/model does not match listing
- seller pushes delivery only

Test:
1. Shoot near and far subjects.
2. Test autofocus repeatedly.
3. Test at widest aperture and stopped down.
4. Shine phone flashlight through lens for fungus/haze.
5. Inspect front/rear glass.
6. Check aperture blades.
7. Check mount contacts.
8. Check exterior for drop damage.

## Guitar pedal / music gear inspection

Bring:
- 9V adapter or battery if needed
- small cable/headphones/portable amp if possible

Reject if:
- cannot test powered on
- bypass/on switch fails
- knobs crackle heavily
- jacks cut out
- battery corrosion
- fake/clone sold as original
- missing critical PSU/cable if not priced in

## Lego/collectible inspection

Prefer sealed items.

Reject if:
- “nepilnas” / missing pieces
- no instructions/box when value depends on it
- opened collectible with no count verification
- suspicious counterfeit signs
- smoke/mold smell
- stolen-looking bulk lots

## General safety

- Meet in public/safe place.
- Do not carry all capital visibly.
- Test before paying.
- Do not use delivery-only sellers for the buy side.
- If margin is not obvious, skip.
